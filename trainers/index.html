Trainers

Laurie Henderson (CPT)

Head Trainer, Owner

  • ACSM Certified Personal Trainer
  • Master's of Science - Kent State University
  • Bachelor's of Science - Central Michigan University
  • MUSC Healthy Charleston Challenge - Team Trainer
  • High School Girls Varsity Basketball Coach- Hanahan

Laurie has been training/coaching individuals of all ages for 12 years. Since her time as captain of the Central Michigan women's basketball team, Laurie has had the desire and capability to inspire and motivate others to strive to achieve their very best. Laurie specializes in group training, fitness boot camps, sport-specific/speed & agility training.

Laurie is skilled in training individuals of all fitness levels. She has trained athletes to individuals that are looking to lose significant weight. Laurie also completed a practicum in her graduate studies which focused on exercise for the elderly.

Laurie believes that for her clients to achieve true wellness they must adopt a "healthy lifestyle." To do this, she feels she must give her clients all the "tools" and knowledge they need today to improve their health for life.

Jason Bremer

Trainer

  • Certified Spinning Instructor
  • Former Semi-Proffesional Cyclist

Jason leads an active lifestyle while remaining fluent in today's technology without compromising what means most to him; his family. Jason has been a well rounded athlete since childhood, even becoming an accomplished Semi-Professional Cyclist while supporting his family as a Graphic Designer in New York City.

Since moving back to Charleston in 2007, he has become a certified spinning instructor, a college professor and an entrepreneur. Despite his many obligations he can still often be found downtown running a 10k, in West Ashley on the Soccer Field or on Isle of Palms on a surfboard. Jason radiates a balance in life that is both inspiring and contagious.
